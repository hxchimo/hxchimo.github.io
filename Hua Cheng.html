HUA CHENG

花城

 Hua Cheng, also referred to as San Lang, is the deuteragonist 
and main love interest in Heaven Official's Blessing. 
He's a ghost of Supreme rank and the most notorious
of the Four Great Calamities, widely known as 
Crimson Rain Sought Flower 

“To me, the you in your greatest glory is you, 
the you that has fallen from grace is also you, 
the most important thing is you, 
not what kind of you.”
