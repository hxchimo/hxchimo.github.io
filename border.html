This acclaimed animated tale by director Hayao Miyazaki follows schoolgirl Satsuke 
and her younger sister, Mei, as they settle into an old country house with their father 
and wait for their mother to recover from an illness in an area hospital. 
As the sisters explore their new home, they encounter 
and befriend playful spirits in their house and the nearby forest, 
most notably the massive cuddly creature known as Totoro.
